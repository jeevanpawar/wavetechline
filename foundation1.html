Go Green

Going green is important for environmental protection. However, it's simply not true. Everything that you do as part of your routine also has an effect on the world's resources. The water you use to brush your teeth to the post-it note on the refrigerator. While some of these ideas may be extreme and greatly affect the way that you perform everyday activities, you can make small changes and spread the word about going green for the environment.
Going green is a movement that challenges others to make the world better. You can conserve natural resources, change your watering habits, and learn more about protecting ecosystems. The planet doesn't belong to the minority, and yet the majority of its resources are being consumed by a small percentage of the population. There is no reason to live this way with as much information that we know. The only reasoning that we have to not join the green effort is that our daily comfort levels may be affected.

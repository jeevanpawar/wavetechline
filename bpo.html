BPO / KPO



India is well known and well established in KPO/BPO services and so is Wave TechLine in Nashik, India. The company is offering KPO/BPO services to main leading companies from US and Japan. This includes wide range of services mentioned as under:


KPO/BPO Services

• Telephonic/Online Interviews

• Data collection, validation, analysis,

• Internet research including profile generation, country reports, product updates, etc.

• Presentation and report generation

• Front end and back end services

• Call center services (24/7)

• Customer Relationship Management

• Managing quiz competition and promo events

• Information Technology enabled services

The company has experienced researchers and executives having work experience of companies in national and international reputes.


CATI/CAPI/CAWI/PAPI and on-line Interviewing Services

The main advantage of working with Wave TechLine is that our origins are those of a full service agency having moved into providing outsourcing for global clients. Therefore we have a strong domain understanding of market research that enables us to add substantial value to our MR outsourcing projects. Our teams of experience research executive have expertise in market analytics, charting, presentation and report writing across all industry verticals. We cater to Indian as well as International clients.

Wave TechLine offers outbound CATI interviewing from our Pune and Nashik Office covering entire India to speaking in English and all major Indian languages. The company conducts both B2C and B2B studies. We have experience in completing projects on consumer products, banking, insurance, telecom, automobile, industrial products, health care etc.. Wave TechLine has experienced research associates who are efficient and have full understanding of telephonic interviews, project management and quality assurance team manages overall operations for the project, briefing, training, quality checks and data delivery. Extensive training of the associates is carried out for language proficiency, understanding of the questionnaire, background on the study, and etiquettes.

All interviewers, supervisors, project managers and quality team are fully trained to the market research industry's IQCS standards and adhere to the standards set by MRSI and ESOMAR code of conduct. During training, the interviewers are further briefed on various topics including the importance of accurate and ethical interviewing. Probing is done and comments and views of the respondents are also gathered tactically.

The data collection aspect through CATI is closely monitored for both voice and data quality by separate in-house team specialized for the same. To a minimum of 15% to 20% percent of all completed interviews are monitored by our internal quality assurance team in various formats such as Voice Checks, Back Checks, Real Time Checks and Data Validation Checks. In addition, all interviewers are monitored at least once in every three shifts. Recordings are provided to the clients on request.

Our quality parameters include voice check that covers checking critical errors such as leading, biasing, skipping, adherence to the text etc. and non critical errors such as probing, soft skills etc. Back check covers checking the screeners and quality of the interview with the respondent. In data quality parameters, the details of the data-verification of pattern, branching, output format, accuracy of data, cleaning of the data as per the specifications, etc. are thoroughly checked.

Wave TechLine is the leading provider of outsourcing solutions for market research data processing and tabulation services. Wave TechLine teams experienced in market research techniques handle data processing and tabulation for clients across the globe, completing thousands of data processing and tabulation projects every year. Our analysts have expertise across industry-standard packages and tools such as SAS, Quantum, SPSS, SPSS Dimensions, Wincross, and others.

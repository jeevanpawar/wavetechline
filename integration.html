Enterprise Integration

Enterprise content management solutions are implemented with an objective to provide a unified portal across the organization, thus providing a single & simple access to organizations information/assets. In addition to this, an organization is bound to have focused solution implementations for Customer Relation Management, Business Intelligence & Reporting, Enterprise Resource Planning and then some custom legacy systems developed in-house for certain divisions.

                 An enterprise will need to integrate these systems with their enterprise level portal and deliver data/content from                these systems via the enterprise portal. Wave TechLine has good understanding of how these external solutions                are implemented and further also has experience in integrating these systems with the chosen platform for the                enterprise portal. Wave TechLine also has its center of excellence for CRM and BI, which the ECM leverages for                   these integrations.

                 Key Areas of Expertise:
                 1. Integration with Business Intelligence (BI) solutions.
                 2. Integrate with Reporting Systems and Platforms.
                 3. Integrate with Customer Relationship Management (CRM) products
                 4. Integrate with Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software.
                 5. Integrate with custom and legacy systems.

                 Fast Facts:
                 1. Focused CRM Center of Excellence to assist in CRM integrations.
                 2. Focused BI Center of Excellence to assist in integration with reporting and BI tools.

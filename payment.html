Payments & Frauds

As E-Commerce markets present opportunities for business to extend beyond geographies and beyond niche demography , payment solutions play a key role in determining the span of the market at the disposal of E-Commerce providers. Both Local and Global E-Commerce today is a key function of an enterprises ability to transact in local currencies, in preferred local payment methods and with solutions that progressively win shopper confidence in providing a secure, seamless online shopping experience.

Fraud presents another key challenge for E-Commerce business to deal with today. Traditionally "revenues lost to fraud" & "cost to keep fraud within reasonable levels" have been accepted as a realistic "cost in business" and organizations have worked to maintain fraud to acceptable levels focussing on their core business. The changing times however are pressing commerce providers to rethink their approach on fraud and to look at it as an opportunity to recover lost margins. The solutions market today is beaming with a verity of solutions, providers stack that presents potential to realize this objective, however choosing appropriate solutions relevant to the business and fine tuning the operations risk management to realise these goals still presents a significant challenge.

Wave TechLine brings extensive engineering and business process expertise in developing and integrating payments solutions for payment solutions and for E-Commerce solution providers. Our experience includes expertise in both Card Present and Card Not Present solutions and is over a wide array of payment methods and payment solutions including solutions on Micro payments, Electronic valets, and Payment processing & transaction settlement solutions. We also bring strong understanding of standards, local regulations and compliances as they apply to payment solutions. Our evolved expertise in testing of payment solutions leverages our extensive experience of web services testing using specialized industry standard and open source tools.

Wave TechLine also brings extensive expertise in analysing and understanding Fraud management needs of E-Commerce providers and in developing integrations with various industry leading fraud management solutions providers along with expertise in developing custom Operations Risk Management solutions to track and minimize fraudulent transactions and to realize Operations Risk Management goals. Our payments and fraud expertise is complemented by a full life cycle offering of Business Support Operations services that cover all customer support and back office support needs to cater to end-to-end needs of payments solutions & E-Commerce solution providers

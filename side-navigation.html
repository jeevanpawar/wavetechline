COMPANY OVERVIEW

WAVE TECHLINE is part of the WAVE Group and is a leading global systems integrator and business transformation consulting organization, focused primarily on the industry and businesses.

The Wave Group employs more than 100 people in all over INDIA and operates in key industries that drive economic growth, enjoying a leadership position in Information Technology, Import Export, Transportation, Agriculture. Wave TechLine expanded its IT portfolio in 2010 by acquiring the leading global business and information technology services company Wave Technology.

In 2012, Wave TechLine featured on the INDIAN GOVERMENTAL list, a listing of the biggest and most powerful company in the Maharashtra.

Wave TechLine's capabilities spread across a broad spectrum, including IT Solutions, Business Support Systems, Operations Support Systems, Network Design & Engineering, Next Generation Networks, Mobility Solutions, The solutions portfolio includes Consulting, Application Development & Management, Network Services, Solution Integration, Product Engineering, comprises BPO, Services and Consulting. With an array of service offerings for governmental Bodies is a chosen transformation partner for several leading IT Solutions Companies in India

Wave TechLine has successfully implemented more than 10 cloud based software product in various industries all over the globe. That includes Enterprise Resources' Planning (ERP), Customer relationship management (CRM), Point of Sale (POS), Electronic Health Care records (EHCR), My Campus Connect cloud based Education institution administration software, election manager and many more. Its development centres' are ISO 9001:2008, ISO 20000, certified.

Supply Chain Implementation

SCI

Supply Chain Execution deals with the movement, storage, and distribution of goods through the entire supply chain. Globalization of manufacturing and outsourcing strategy has given tremendous advantage to organizations enabling them to focus on their core competency. However, it has introduced new challenges in monitoring, analyzing, and responding to supply chain events.

Mobile Supply Chain Execution solutions have gained wide acceptance with extensive use of RFID, Barcode, GPS, and mobile applications. SCM ISVs across the globe are developing innovative wireless and mobile applications and are extending support to related platforms.

Leading logistics IT solutions providers for 3PLs, fleet owners, and distributors have partnered with Wave TechLine to develop, maintain, and test their flagship products.

Testing And QA

Total commitment to quality and customer satisfaction forms the key factors for any organization to be successful. Wave TechLine believes in providing high-quality deliverables through its "Quality Assurance" and "Quality Process" programs.


High Quality has now become the fulcrum on which the success of a product depends. In this rapidly changing business marketplace where competition is fierce and dynamic, it is essential for a product to exhibit world-class quality to survive and grow. How good a product works, how easy it is to use, and how fast it performs are key decision points for customers in deciding which product to engage with. This is where Testing and QA play a decisive role.

Wave TechLine has a mature and well-set Testing practice with a strong team, doing Verification & Validation of some of the most complex products. Our QA clientele comprises the crème-de-la-crème of the world's top IT and product-based companies. With well-entrenched QA processes, in-depth knowledge about the latest testing tools and technologies, and strong technical QA leadership, Wave TechLine is well positioned to contribute effectively towards your Testing needs.

Quick Facts

1000 strong team of skilled, passionate, and creative Test engineers

Proven expertise in industry's leading testing tools such as HP Quick Test Pro, Win Runner, Rational Robot, and Silk Test.

Strong capabilities in emerging open source testing tools such as Fitnesse, Selenium, and Watir, among others.

State-of-the-art automation solutions for completing automating testing lifecycle – from build deployment and smoke testing to functional and regression testing in various tools and technologies

Strong exposure in developing custom tools and harnesses for specific client testing requirements in a variety of technologies such as Perl, C, C++, C#, Java, Python, and Ruby, among others.

Our breadth and depth of testing encompasses tools, technologies, methodologies, and domain-specific knowledge to create the most optimal solutions for your business

Mature and well-set processes to execute Testing projects in various methodologies such as Agile/Scrum, RAD, TDD, and Waterfall.

Proven solutions and expertise to speed up the testing cycle and reduce the time-to-market.

In-house Testing CoE (Centre of Excellence) for fostering knowledge sharing within the Testing practice as well as research and adaptation of the emerging testing tools and technologies.

Operational Support

Operations management and customer support form the back bone of e-commerce operations. The integrations and multi-party liaisons oriented nature of the domain along with the high end-consumer experience requirements make support operations a key ingredient in the overall e-commerce solution offering. The specialized requirements around handling of sensitive financial data, stringent TAT and QOS requirements require support operations to be reviewed and redesigned considering the specialized needs of commerce solution providers. The function in itself requires a sizable focus, unique infrastructure, operations methodology and specialized expertise that it has the ability to quickly morph into an unsustainable cost centre or a bottleneck to scalability.

Wave TechLine brings extensive experience around customer support services to the table which includes a full array of support services from Level 1 through Level 4 over voice, text and email. The specialized dedicated infrastructure, evolved operations support processes, focussed quality control and rich experience in supporting customers in the e-commerce and payment solutions space enables the group to provide end-to-end support services that meet full life cycle support needs. The support operations are designed with specific focus on the data sensitivity and is supported by a team of practice architects that help develop tools, data transformation solutions and processes to enable rapid compliance with PCI-DSS standards.
